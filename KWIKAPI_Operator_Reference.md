## üìä Operator Status Legend

- **Active (1):** Operator is currently operational
- **Inactive (0):** Operator is temporarily unavailable

## üîç Bill Fetch Legend

- **YES:** Bill fetching is supported - use `/bills/validation.php` before payment
- **NO:** Direct recharge/payment without bill fetch

## üè¶ BBPS Legend

- **YES:** BBPS (Bharat Bill Payment System) enabled
- **NO:** Non-BBPS operator

---

## üìù Important Notes

1. **Account Field Variations:** Different operators require different account number formats
2. **Optional Parameters:** Some operators require opt1, opt2, etc. for additional information
3. **Amount Limits:** Always check min/max amounts before transaction
4. **Bill Fetch:** Only use bill fetch for operators with Bill Fetch = YES
5. **BBPS Compliance:** BBPS-enabled operators follow NPCI guidelines
